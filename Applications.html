Applications

Since EEG belongs to time series data, all the algorithm we implement can be used for different purpose. We can for example use apply approach to analyse and give a sens to financial curves and find some patterns.

Our main goal is to focus on medical application. We tend to develop a light method which can be used on small and cheap devices. This restraint ensure us to be realistic concerning the portability of the deice. As a matter of fact, a patient can not transport a bulky one in everyday life.

If we try to prevent and warn the user that he may have an epilepsy crisis or an heart attack, the device should work online and can not (like SpiNN) work when we have all the data offline.

This approach could link the will to the movement of someone wearing a prothesis. For instance if we can translate what the user think and transform his thought into an electric signal to his prothesis, it may move like he wanted to. As you can imagine there's an infinity of application of such a portable device which would enhance our life.
